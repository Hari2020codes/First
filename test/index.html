Character Descriptions

Iron Man (Tony Stark)
Billionaire genius inventor who created the Iron Man armor after being captured by terrorists. His character arc spans from selfish playboy to ultimate hero who sacrifices himself to save the universe.
Captain America (Steve Rogers)
A frail young man from Brooklyn who was transformed into a super soldier during World War II. Known for his unwavering moral compass and leadership skills.
Thor
The Asgardian God of Thunder, initially arrogant and reckless, who learns humility and becomes a worthy hero. Wields the mighty hammer Mjolnir and later the axe Stormbreaker.
